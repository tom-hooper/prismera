Managed IT Services

Keep your business running smoothly with proactive IT management from our Melbourne-based support team. We monitor your systems around the clock, resolving issues before they disrupt your operations. From helpdesk support and hardware procurement through to vendor management, we handle the day-to-day technology demands so your team can focus on what they do best. Our managed services scale with your business, ensuring you always have the right level of support without the overhead of an in-house IT department.

  • 24/7 proactive system monitoring and alerting
  • Responsive helpdesk support with Australian-based engineers
  • Hardware procurement, configuration and lifecycle management
  • Vendor relationship management across your technology stack
  • Regular health checks and performance reporting

Cybersecurity

Protect your business from evolving cyber threats with a comprehensive security framework built on the Australian Signals Directorate's Essential Eight. We combine advanced threat detection with endpoint protection and network security to create multiple layers of defence around your critical data. Our team continuously monitors your environment, responds to incidents in real time, and keeps you ahead of regulatory requirements. Whether you are pursuing ISO 27001 certification or simply want peace of mind, we design a security posture that matches your risk profile.

  • Essential Eight maturity assessments and compliance roadmaps
  • Endpoint detection and response (EDR) across all devices
  • Network security including firewalls, segmentation and VPNs
  • Security awareness training and phishing simulations for staff
  • Incident response planning and breach containment

Cloud Solutions

Unlock the full potential of the cloud with solutions designed for Australian businesses. We manage your Microsoft 365 environment, migrate workloads to Azure, and implement robust cloud backup strategies that keep your data safe and accessible. For organisations not yet ready to go fully cloud-native, we architect hybrid environments that bridge on-premises infrastructure with cloud services - giving you flexibility without compromise. Every deployment is built with security, compliance and cost optimisation at its core.

  • Microsoft 365 tenant setup, migration and ongoing administration
  • Azure cloud migration - lift-and-shift, refactor or re-architect
  • Cloud backup and disaster recovery with tested failover
  • Hybrid infrastructure design connecting on-premises and cloud
  • Cloud cost optimisation and licence management
