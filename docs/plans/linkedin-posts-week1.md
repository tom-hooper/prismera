## Post 2: Ransomware Reality Check (Wednesday AM)

Here's what ransomware actually costs a Melbourne SMB:

💰 $180k+ in downtime, recovery, and reputation damage
⏱️ Average 21 days before you're back to normal
📉 60% of small businesses that get hit close within 6 months

And here's the part most people miss:
You don't get hit because someone "hacked" you.
You get hit because a staff member opened an attachment that looked legitimate.

The fix isn't expensive.
- Tested backups (not just "we think they're running")
- MFA on everything
- Email filtering that catches the clever ones
