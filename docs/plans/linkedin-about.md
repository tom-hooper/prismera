## About / Description

Prismera is a Melbourne-based managed IT services provider that brings enterprise-grade technology within reach of Australian small and medium businesses.

Most growing companies are stuck between two bad options: overpriced corporate IT providers they can't afford, and consumer-grade tools that leave them exposed. We built Prismera to bridge that gap.

What we do:
- Managed IT Services — proactive support, Australian-based helpdesk, hardware lifecycle management
- Cybersecurity — Essential Eight-aligned, 24/7 threat monitoring, endpoint protection, incident response
- Cloud Solutions — Microsoft 365 management, Azure migration, hybrid infrastructure, backup and disaster recovery
- Digital Strategy & vCIO — technology roadmaps, IT budgeting, board-ready reporting

Why Melbourne businesses choose us:
- 100% Australian owned and operated — our support team is right here, no offshore runarounds
- No lock-in contracts — we earn your business every month
- Vendor-agnostic advice — we recommend what's right for your business, not what earns us the highest margin
- Transparent pricing — clear, predictable monthly costs

Whether you're a five-person startup or an established enterprise with hundreds of staff, we protect, power, and future-proof your technology so you can focus on what you do best.
